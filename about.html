Tales of a Broke College Student

College on a Budget -The Real, The Ratchet, The Reckless

Students taking out loans, and the struggle getting realer with every semester beginning and ending, many of us are trying to find every damn way to cope and survive. Especially in cities like New York and San Fransisco, the struggle can be exceptionally real, given the rising costs of rent, public transportation (damn, MTA) and living expenses. This blog was inspired by the experiences of college students in New York and their stories of how to go to school, work, enjoy the college experience, all whilst being on the lowest of budgets. Not only is this a place to record the history of the new era of the American college experience, but to share the victories, and losses, of how we made it through.
